### Currently

I am a Member of Technical Staff on the Pretraining Team at Wayfarer Labs, where I train foundational interactive video models and scale up data and inference pipelines for video diffusion models. My paper [COMBAT: Conditional World Models for Behavioral Agent Training]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/publications) was accepted at the 1st RIWM workshop at ICCV 2025.

Previously, I was a Research Intern at the [Vision and AI Lab](https://val.cds.iisc.ac.in/), Indian Institute of Science (IISc). See the [About]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/about) page for details, or browse my [projects]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/software).
